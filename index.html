What is Valo4Noobs?

Valo4Noobs is a educational webiste for Valorant, a Tactical FPS game developed by Riot Games which launched in 2020.

Check out Guides to deepen your knowledge on topics such as map control and gun hygiene, as well as up-to-date tech for your favourite agent. Learn more about the pro scene at the Esports page, or try your luck at one of our quizzes under Games. Lastly, if you are curious, learn more about the creator of Valo4Noobs under the About page. Who knows what you will learn!
